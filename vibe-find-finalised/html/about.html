About Vibefind

Discover the story behind our mission to connect people through events

Our Story

Vibefind was born out of a simple insight: finding and attending events that truly match your interests shouldn't be difficult.

In 2023, three friends - Samudyatha, Akhil, and Nilesh - were frustrated with existing event platforms that made it hard to discover relevant events. They envisioned a platform where people could easily find events that resonated with their interests and cultural preferences.

What started as a small project quickly gained traction as more people discovered how easy it was to find events they truly enjoyed. Today, Vibefind has become one of India's fastest-growing event discovery platforms, connecting thousands of attendees with events that match their vibe.

Our mission remains the same: to create meaningful connections between people and experiences. We believe that every event has the potential to create lasting memories and meaningful connections, and we're committed to making those experiences accessible to everyone.
