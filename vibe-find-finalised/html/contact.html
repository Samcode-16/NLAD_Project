Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

You can create an account by clicking on the "Sign Up" button in the top-right corner of any page. Fill in your details, solve the CAPTCHA, and you're good to go!

How do I book tickets for an event?

To book tickets, navigate to the event page by clicking on any event card. On the event details page, click the "Book Now" button and follow the instructions to complete your purchase.

Can I create my own events?

Yes! After signing in, you can navigate to the "Create Event" page where you can fill in all the details about your event, upload images, and publish it for others to discover.

How do I save events to my wishlist?

To save an event to your wishlist, simply click the heart icon on any event card. You can view all your saved events by clicking on "My Wishlist" in your profile dropdown menu.

How can I get a refund for a cancelled event?

If an event is cancelled, refunds are processed automatically within 7-10 business days. If you haven't received your refund after this period, please contact our support team at support@vibefind.com.
